WHO WE ARE

We are a group of highly dedicated and well informed persons who wants to bring a change in the Society through the weapon of EDUCATION , a divine blessings, present in all human beings on this beautiful cosmos ,only we have to do is to ignite it through education. We are registered NGO under HARYANA ACT 2012 with Regd NO. HR018201401568 allotted on 20 OCT 2014.


WHY WE STARTED

We believe that we have shared responsibility to eradicate the illiteracy from the society to reduce their sufferings due to lack of knowledge/information. To return to the society , what we have received from it in the form of our well being.


WHAT WE DO

Keeping in mind the importance of education, we started out noble journey on 2nd of October 2014 with a small group of four children, with a little resource of slate/chalk and black board, in open, near by my residence with the vision of ensuring education for the underprivileged children of our society. Slowly but steadily , Step by step, we tried to cover a large number of orphaned / under privileged children of our vicinity in Gurugram(Haryana). We prepare these roaming children with the basic knowledge/learning and get them admitted in nearby Govt.School according to their age, after preparing their necessary documents, free of charge.

Apart from education, we spread awareness amongst the society about social benefit available for general population. Provide medical support, help in marriage of poor girls, helps in performance of last rites of poor deceased person.

Encourage youth to come forward and join Armed Force for the sake and safety of our motherland. We also impart education to our children regarding process of admission in Sainik School/Military school. Impart Legal knowledge , career counseling etc.
